Cookies are small text files which are stored on your computer by your web browser. 
There are two types of Cookie known as session Cookies and persistent Cookies. 
A session Cookie is a temporary file which is stored in your computer for the duration of your visit to a website. 
The Cookie is deleted when you leave the site. 
A persistent Cookie is saved to your computer and will remain there for the duration set within the Cookie.
